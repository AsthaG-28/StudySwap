How it Works

STUDYSWAP is a simple, student-friendly platform that makes it easy to exchange academic resources. Whether you're looking for study guides, textbooks, lecture notes, or past exam papers, STUDYSWAP lets you connect with other students to trade materials.

Simply CREATE an account.

UPLOAD the resources you're offering.

And BROWSE through available listings to find what you need.

When you find a match, you can ARRANGE A SWAP OR SHARE resources directly.

It's a hassle-free way to share knowledge and make the most of what you and your peers have to offer.
